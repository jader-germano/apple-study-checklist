# ``AppleStudyChecklist``

`AppleStudyChecklist` is a local-first SwiftUI study workspace for macOS and iOS.

## Overview

The app combines:

- a checklist-driven study program
- markdown-backed content and labels
- a vault workspace that can be bundled, local, or external
